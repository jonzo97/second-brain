## Key Trends (Feb 2026)

1. **MoE LLMs from Chinese labs** are the hottest category (MiniMax, GLM-5, Qwen3.5, Kimi, Ring)
2. **TTS and audio** surging (MOSS-TTS, Qwen3-TTS, PersonaPlex, SoulX-Singer)
3. **MCP integration** appearing as standard tags on spaces and datasets
4. **Robotics datasets** strong emerging cluster (NVIDIA, Allen AI, Snowflake)
5. **Video generation** still hot - Wan2.2 dominating spaces
6. **Agentic AI for research** accumulating significant attention
